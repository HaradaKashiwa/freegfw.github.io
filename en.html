FreeGFW

To allow more people to access the internet. A high-performance proxy service management system based on Sing-box and Xray core.

Decentralized Mesh Network

Unique Feature

FreeGFW features an innovative Link functionality. Connect multiple nodes to form a private, decentralized network. This lowers the risk of single point failures and mitigates blocking.

  • Subscription Aggregation: Subscribe to one node to get all interconnected nodes.
  • Auto-Sync: Server configurations continuously synchronize automatically.
  • Decentralization: Any two points can establish trust without a central server.
"Ideal for private proxy networks designed for groups of friends or families wanting maximum uptime."
